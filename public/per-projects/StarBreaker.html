StarBreaker is a mecha rougelike. Check out the github for more information on the current state of the project.

 

1/8/2024 - History of development so far

It was Jan 3, I had been playing Twenty Minuites Till Dawn and I was thinking of what I would make if I were to make a game. I rembered playing bubble tanks, in particular bubble tanks 2 and how I liked the way that you could choose which direction you wanted to evolve your tank.

I had watched Gurren Lagan the week before and I got the idea to combine all of these ideas together and make a mecha rougelike.

I reached out to my friend Rowan Peters who was (and as of now still is) majoring in game design with the idea.

I've added the ideas that I've come up with to the github, and plan on coninuing work when I have more time, probably this summer.
